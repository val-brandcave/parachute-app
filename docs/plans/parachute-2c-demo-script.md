# Parachute v2 — Inline Review Demo Script (Phase 2c)

**Purpose:** the canonical walkthrough for Looms and live demos of the inline-editing model across BOTH review types. Written so the demo **never opens Customize** (plan §9, 2c) — the story is speed and defensibility, not configuration.

**Cast:** one review ordered with **Technical + Administrative** (the confirm gate's default — "80% order both").

---

## 0. Setup (before recording)

- Fresh session, light theme, standalone mode (`/launchpad`).
- Have a source file ready to drop (any PDF — the demo parses a mock).
- Close every panel; you should never show Customize or the Builder.

## 1. Intake → confirm → processing (~30s)

1. Drop the appraisal on the Launchpad hero. The run takeover opens on the **confirm gate**: parsed identity, both review types pre-checked. *"Nothing to configure — Parachute read the report; you confirm and go."*
2. **Start review.** Let the pipeline stages play. *"This is the long part in production. You leave; we notify you."*

## 2. Technical — the 30-second review (~90s)

Land on the **Workbook**. This is the beat that answers Jeff's *"if I gave you 30 seconds… could you fix this stuff in two minutes?"*

1. Point at the callout: *"N items need a closer look."* Click **Review them** — the document scrolls to the first undecided finding **in the doc**. You never leave the deliverable.
2. **Concur** one finding — one click, decided line appears with your name and time.
3. **Edit** another — the block flips into the composer where it stands, reword it, save. Point at the **"Rewritten by reviewer"** provenance. *"A regulator sees the human touched it, on the artifact itself."*
4. **Delete** a finding — then scroll to the Conclusion: *"excluded but retained in the audit log — nothing silently disappears."* (Restore it if you want the round trip.)
5. The crayons: click a **narrative paragraph**, type a sentence, Done. **Delete Comparable 5** from the comp grid; the $/SF exhibit re-derives live. *"You can't screw it up — the table never breaks."*
6. Open **Activity** (toolbar): the complete ledger — every click, before → after. Close it.
7. Flip **Edit | Preview**: *"exactly what the bank receives."* Flip back.
8. **Sign & finalize** — type the name, seal. Point at the SHA-256.

## 3. Administrative — the same muscle memory (~60s)

Switch to the **Administrative** tab (it pre-processed in the background).

1. You land on the **attestation document** — the bank's own checklist, compiled and pre-filled. *"Same idea: the document is the workspace."*
2. Point at the callout split: *"22 items — most are routine, a few need judgment."* Click the inline **"attest them in one click"** in the callout sentence — the AI-confident, unchanged items attest at once, on the paper.
3. Click **Review them** — the doc scrolls to a **Needs attention** row and opens it: AI's suggestion, confidence, the quoted evidence. Click a **different answer** — the reason field opens (required for the audit trail). Type why, **Confirm change**. The row now prints *"Changed from Yes — …"* right on the form.
4. One more row: click the **suggested answer** — attested in one click. *"Yes is one click; disagreeing costs one sentence."*
5. (Optional) **Source** in the rail: the original appraisal with every item's cited span highlighted — same answers, same store, both surfaces always agree.
6. Open **Activity** — the admin ledger mirrors the technical one.
7. **Sign attestation** — seal it. Both types signed; the run can finish.

## 4. The deliverables (~20s)

- **Download** on either signed document → print-to-PDF of the exact sheets you worked on. *"What you reviewed is what they receive."*
- Close: *"Two reviews, one sitting, every decision on the document, every touch audited."*

## Never do in this demo

- Open **Customize** (the 20% stays behind its button).
- Open the **Builder** or Templates.
- Show dark mode with a document open (documents are always paper).
- Use the word "regenerate" — direct edits are live; the only compile beat is the system's.
